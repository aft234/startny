TRADESHOW

Silicon Alley has grown to represent the startup ecosystem blossoming in the shadows of New York's major landmarks. Yet "The Alley" isn't a physical place. It's a distributed network of entrepreneurs and organizations striving to make an imprint on a city traditionally dominated by titans and well-funded incumbents.

So how can a startup reach their target audience and how can people from the outside connect with startups? Ideally STARTNY is the event to create a million conversations for each of the groups to meet. We’re inviting 500 companies and 10,000. Whatever you’re looking to discover will be present.

ONE-ON-ONES

Everybody has a plan until they get punched in the face. Creating and launching a successful product requires the same resiliency. We've asked some of our favorite programmers, designers, and investors to sit with attendees to discuss their startups. But don't expect empty compliments. The feedback will be intentionally critical to see whether you can get off the mat.
