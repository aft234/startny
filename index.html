STARTUP SHOWCASE

On April 4, Silicon Alley is taking over the Javits Center for the largest and best startup event in city history! Thousands of people will gather to network and discover what the startup hype is all about. A million introductions will take place, so make sure you're part of the conversation!

Attendees: Purchase a ticket for an all-access pass. You'll join 10,000 others including investors, press, and loads of hiring companies. Tickets are 50% off ($10) for the first 1,000 attendees!

Startups: Exhibit your company to the largest gathering of consequential people your product can possibly get in front of. Why tweet from behind a computer when you can meet your targets face-to-face? Apply above.

ONE-ON-ONES

Everybody has a plan until they get punched in the face. New product launches are no different, and the ones that make it know how to get up off the mat. We've asked some of our favorite programmers, designers, and strategists to sit with attendees to provide actionable feedback on their startups. But don't expect empty compliments. Volunteers participating in one-on-ones have been instructed to take the gloves off with attendees to give the most constructive advice possible.

PANELS

It's hard to keep up with what's trending in the startup world unless you spend all day skimming Reddit. The amount of new information to consume is astoundingly large, and with so much noise it's nearly impossible to determine which advice is worthwhile to follow. Our panelists consist of the top leaders in investing, company formation, startup recruitment, and the New York startup ecosystem, and will share their perspectives on a range of compelling issues.
