SHOWCASE

We're giving New York a unique startup experience unlike any other. Not only is the scale and potential of the Javits Center astounding, we're infusing a super slate of workshops, classes, office hours, and networking events to help you connect with some of the top leaders in the startup community. There's no better place to be whether you're at a startup or just startup-curious.

ONE-ON-ONES

We've all had a million dollar idea but didn't know what to do! Honestly, it's hard to find the right person to talk to. That's why we've asked some of the top developers, designers and strategists to hold 15-minute one-on-one sessions with attendees all day long. This way you'll be on the top of your game when Shark Tank calls!

WORKSHOPS

You know what they say, great entrepreneurs are made not born. No matter how endowed you are as an entrepreneur you can still become better. Our workshops are geared toward both beginner and experienced entrepreneurs to teach the basics as well as advanced techniques that can raise your startup game.

HACKATHONS

Hackathon is a pretty weird word if you are not at a tech startup! But that's the purpose of START NY, to create the place where the startup community converges with the outside world. Our hackathons will be packed with developers and fueled by Red Bull. Both will be announced in February, but one involving cryptography and virtual currency is rumored to be in the works. Register below to get notified when they are announced.

PANELS

Did you say Content Advertising? Growth Hacking? The Internet of Things? These terms even make us scratch our heads. So we're inviting domain experts to explain them in plain English. Other panels will be more basic in nature to help people recruit for startup jobs, start a company, and connect with the NYC tech community. If you don't know, now you know.

JOBS PORTAL

It's 2014 people – Craigslist is not the answer! Gain an edge on recruiting talent and scoring your dream startup job at the main event and on our HUGE online jobs portal. Exhibiting startups can post openings that they're recruiting for and registered attendees can reach out to say, "Hey, I'm interested." Couldn't be easier, but you've got to register to participate.
